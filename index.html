Road to Fate

Hopes are dashed to smithereens
The blind men are on a futile chase
Pittance are given to workers
Yet they carve a smile on their face.

Wishes of a country stepped upon by wicked apes
Grins and laughter fills the room of their sacred safes
Bowing Irokos laughing at Human greed
Soils are waiting to end our mischief.

Now the sun is bright and the Apes are gone
Safes are stolen by the one who knows all
With a feathered ink I write an ode creed
Creed to be sung for our leaders in the seat of mischief.

Witches Tale

Call me a sea gull, for many galls have i eaten.
When they lie in the night, i ring a bell for our meeting.
Worthless humans, worthless souls
What i cherish most is their blood in a bowl

They cast and bind, yet they fall into my trap
I send my girls to them, and they try to see in-between their laps
Have you not heard of my prowess from your ancestors foot?
I am a fortress , I destroy you from your root

Oh! How we miss those fresh sacrifices
Your ancestors were wiser, they paid their taxes
Who shall save you from our clutches
Weak humans hardly believing their doctrines.
